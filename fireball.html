Frederik Exner
FIREBALL

Swarming fly
Come rest with me
What makes you dance
So seamlessly?

Whirring tones
Played by no hand
My wakeful nights
In your command

My visitor
The Lord of Pests
What carry you
Besides from death?


Oh restless fly
Come sit on me
You pure artistic
Agency

-Frederik Exner



Frederik Exner (b. 1991, Denmark) graduated with a BFA from The Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts in 2019. Lives and work in Paris.
